How Night Cricket is Reimagining Fan Culture in Bengaluru

Prime-time fixtures at the Bengaluru Super Sports League now merge spectacle with science. Adaptive lighting rigs, immersive audio beds, and AI-assisted scoring keep players sharp while fans experience every heartbeat of the game.

Community lounges, student innovation hubs, and mixed-reality skill challenges activate the stadium concourse between overs, making the venue a night-long festival rather than a two-hour showdown.

SBSO Foundation’s broadcast partners capture the energy with multi-angle rigs and interactive overlays, pushing Bengaluru’s cricket nights to a global streaming audience hungry for next-gen storytelling.
