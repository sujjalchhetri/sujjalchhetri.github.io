About Us

The SBSO Foundation is committed to promoting fitness and outdoor sports among youth. To continue this vision, we are launching the Bengaluru Super Sports League (BSSL), a turf cricket tournament in T10 format, to be held in November.

BSSL will provide young players with the right platform to showcase their talent while engaging a large live audience. At the same time, it offers great opportunities for sponsors to advertise and reach a highly energetic and connected crowd. In the past, sponsors like The Souled Store have successfully partnered with us.

Our mission is simple—make the youth fit, active, and inspired through outdoor sports while creating value for our players, audiences, and partners.
