Inclusive Cricket: Designing Events for Every Athlete

The Divyang Pro Super League is engineered with accessibility-first thinking—from tactile pitch markers and adaptive dugouts to physio hubs staffed across every innings.

Broadcast partners integrate athlete-led explainers, helping viewers appreciate adaptive tactics while celebrating courage, craft, and community impact.

Design blueprints from the league now inform SBSO Foundation’s wider events, proving that elite competition and universal access can move forward together.
