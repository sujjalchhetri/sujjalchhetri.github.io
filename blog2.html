Building Leadership Pathways for Women Cricketers

Women’s BSSL blends high-performance coaching with mentorship in analytics, operations, and media, helping athletes discover leadership roles beyond the pitch.

Athletes rotate through captaincy labs, match-control simulations, and content studios, ensuring they leave the program with tactical intelligence and communications confidence.

The pathway has already propelled graduates into state squads, corporate leagues, and grassroots academies, proving that investing in leadership multiplies opportunities across Indian sport.
