❓ BSSL FAQs

▶ What is the BSSL (Bangalore Super Sports League)?
BSSL is the Bangalore Super Sports League, a premier sports tournament bringing together top teams and athletes from Bangalore and beyond.
▶ Who can take participate in BSSL?
Anyone can participate, including beginner, amateur and professional players.
▶ How can I register for the league?
You can register online through the official BSSL website or contact the organizers for registration details.
▶ How many teams are part of the tournament?
The number of teams taking participations are 16 in total.
▶ What is the minimum age to participate?
There is no minimum age to participate. Everyone can participate.
